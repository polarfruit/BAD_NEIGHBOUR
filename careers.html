What We're Looking For.

Barista

You know your way around a machine and you care about consistency. Latte art is a bonus, attitude is not optional.

All-Rounder

A bit of everything — food prep, sandwich press, customer service, keeping the place looking sharp. You don't wait to be told what needs doing.

  • Hospitality experience preferred
  • Can handle busy periods
  • Team player with initiative

Kitchen Hand

Prep, clean, restock, repeat. It's not glamorous but it's the backbone of everything we do. We respect the grind.

  • Reliable and punctual
  • Food safety awareness
  • Willing to learn
