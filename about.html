How It Started

A Queue
In London.

2023. Europe trip. We saw a line out the door for chocolate strawberries. People losing their minds over melted chocolate and fruit.

We came home, grabbed a marquee, a tub of melted chocolate, and a few thousand strawberries. Set up at a market. Sold out.

That was Chocolate Boys. A snack idea that blew up fast.

Then It Grew

Delicious
Chaos.

Weekend pop-ups turned into shopping centres. Shopping centres turned into festivals. Festivals turned into a Tuk Tuk rolling up to weddings, birthdays, and events across the country.

We added loaded cookies. Dubai chocolate bars. A spread that people were buying jars of before we could restock.

What started as strawberries at a market became something bigger. Something that needed a home.

What It Became

Bad
Neighbour.

We wanted a permanent spot. Somewhere we could do more than just chocolate. Coffee done right. Sandos built to make you feel something. And yeah, still the chocolate.

Harbour Town Adelaide. We turned a corner into something worth driving to. The kind of place where your mates send you a photo and you're in the car before they finish the caption.

We're not here to be your friend. Come anyway.
